About Jane

Li-Rou Jane Foong is a privacy counsel and published author with a career spanning APAC, Europe, and the Americas. She brings deep technical legal expertise to the increasingly complex terrain of global data protection and AI regulation.

Her legal work centres on the intersection of technology, data governance, and human rights — advising global platforms on compliance, building privacy-by-design frameworks, and navigating multi-jurisdictional regulatory obligations. She is a Fellow of the IAPP and holds both CIPP/E and CIPM certifications.

She is currently completing her LLM in Privacy & Cybersecurity at USC Gould School of Law, with a Certification in Law & Artificial Intelligence.
